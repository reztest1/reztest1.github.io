About Us

Who Are We?

Strategic Business Solutions Ltd. –SBSL is an ICT based solutions company.
It has four focus business areas such as

(i) IT Project Management,
(ii) IT Infrastructure Solutions,
(iii) Software Development Solutions,
(iv) Project Management Training.

Strategic Business Solutions Ltd. –SBSL has been providing IT solutions to the Government, Bank and RMG sectors since its start journey.

We specialize in helping our customers make the most of their IT environments; whether managing and modernizing their Datacenter, Active and Passive Network or helping them incorporate new technologies such as virtualization or cloud computing into their production environments.

We also have expertise to help the customers in IT project management and training like Project Management Professional - PMP.

SBSL believes on “Quantification for Change” and for this reason, entire organization works through a value stream process for measurement and improvement.
